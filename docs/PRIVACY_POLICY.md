# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

This Privacy Policy explains how JejuFlow handles information when you use the JejuFlow mobile app.

## App Overview

JejuFlow is a travel assistant for Jeju Island. It helps users view weather-aware destination suggestions, public transit information, and saved travel routes.

## Information We Access or Use

JejuFlow may access location information if you grant location permission. Location is used to support nearby destination recommendations and travel context inside the app.

JejuFlow stores some app preferences and saved route information locally on your device. This may include selected language, selected region, cached public travel data, and routes you save in the app.

JejuFlow does not require account registration and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, payment information, government ID, health information, photos, videos, or contacts.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used for app functionality, including:

- Showing weather-aware travel recommendations.
- Showing nearby or region-based destination information.
- Saving routes you choose to keep in the app.
- Remembering app preferences such as language.
- Loading public weather, tourism, map, and transit information.

## Sharing

JejuFlow does not sell user data.

JejuFlow does not share your saved routes or app preferences with third parties. The app may request public travel, weather, map, or transit data from external API providers to provide app functionality. These requests are used to retrieve public information for the app experience.

## Ads and Analytics

JejuFlow does not currently show ads.

JejuFlow does not currently use an advertising SDK or a dedicated analytics SDK.

## Data Storage and Retention

Saved routes, cached travel data, and preferences are stored locally on your device. They remain until you delete them in the app where supported, clear app data in Android settings, or uninstall the app.

JejuFlow does not operate a user account system, so there is no separate account deletion process.

## Security

JejuFlow is designed to keep API keys and signing credentials out of the public source repository. Public API requests use HTTPS where supported by the provider.

No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but the app limits user data handling to what is needed for travel functionality.

## Children

JejuFlow is not designed specifically for children. The recommended target audience is users aged 13 and over.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when JejuFlow changes its features, data handling, or release requirements. The updated date above will reflect the latest revision.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support contact listed on the JejuFlow Google Play store listing.
